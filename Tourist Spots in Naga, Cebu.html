In the past, Naga was known as "Narra" due to the abundant Narra trees in the area. Later, it was renamed "Naga" after the Naga (Cobra) River, which snakes its way through the town. Over time, the town became an essential hub for trade and commerce, mainly due to its strategic location near the sea. Naga is now famous for picture-perfect peaks, rolling hills, and a gorgeous boardwalk that attracts thousands of visitors. Indeed, the city is bound for greatness. The City of Naga is located along the southern coast of the Province of Cebu.

Spot 1: Apo Cement Corporation

Apo Cement Corporation is a cement plant located in Naga, Cebu. It is a popular industrial landmark.

Spot 2: Naga Boardwalk

Naga Boardwalk is a scenic promenade along the coast of Naga, offering beautiful views of the sea.

Spot 3: Pasilong sa Naga

Pasilong in Naga is made of interlocking rings that represent circles of life.

Spot 4: Naga City Plaza

Naga City Plaza is a public square in the heart of Naga, frequented by locals and tourists alike.

Spot 5: Mt. Naupa

Mt. Naupa is a popular hiking destination near Naga, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
